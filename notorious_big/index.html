Song analysis: “Juicy”, Notorious B.I.G

He wrote and recorded a record called “Juicy” which was the record which really made Biggie well respected and prosperous. The lyrics in the record Juicy give people struggling, hope. It lets the listeners know that everyone dreams and even people who aren't blessed like others can still make something of their lives.
