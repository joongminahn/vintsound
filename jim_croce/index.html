Why did Jim Croce write "Time in a Bottle"?

"Time in a Bottle" is a hit single by singer-songwriter Jim Croce. Croce wrote the lyrics after his wife Ingrid told him she was pregnant, in December 1970. ... In 1977, "Time in a Bottle" was used as the title for a compilation album of Croce's love songs.
